# Kadro Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## Introduction

Kadro is a mobile application for sharing and discovering creative work, including artwork, photography, and design.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using Kadro.

---

## Information We Collect

When you create or use an account, we may collect information such as:

- Email address
- Display name
- Profile photo
- Biography
- Uploaded images and posts
- Likes and favorites
- Authentication provider information
- App activity required to operate Kadro

Authentication credentials are securely handled by Firebase Authentication and supported sign-in providers.

---

## How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage accounts
- Display profiles and posts
- Upload and display images
- Save likes and favorites
- Improve app reliability
- Respond to support requests
- Prevent abuse and maintain platform safety

---

## Public Content

Posts uploaded to Kadro may be publicly visible to other users, including:

- Images
- Titles
- Descriptions
- Categories
- Public profile information

Please avoid uploading sensitive personal information.

---

## Third-Party Services

Kadro uses third-party services including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore
- Firebase Storage
- Google Sign-In
- Facebook Login

These services may process information necessary to provide Kadro features.

---

## Data Sharing

Kadro does not sell personal information.

Limited information may be shared with service providers required to operate the application or when legally required.

---

## Account Deletion & Data Removal

Users can delete their Kadro account directly from the app settings.

To delete your account:

1. Open Kadro
2. Go to Profile
3. Open Settings
4. Select Delete Account

When an account is deleted, Kadro attempts to remove:

- User profile
- Profile images
- Uploaded posts
- Post images
- Likes and favorites
- Authentication account data

Some information may remain temporarily in backups or where required by law.
